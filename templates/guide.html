What is Anki?

If you do not know what Anki is, check it out first. In short, Anki is a program that uses a special technique to help you remember information.

What is Anki Word Adder?

Anki Word Adder (AWA) connects to your Anki application and allows you to create cards for learning new words in a couple of clicks. Card example:

Limitations

AWA is a fresh project, so it has some limitations that may be removed later on:
  • AWA cannot be used on mobile devices, because it depends on AnkiConnect add-on and add-ons are only available on Windows, Mac and Linux.
  • AWA may be unavailable at the end of each month, because of the free hosting time limit
  • AWA can only create cards for English words. If you want support for other languages, you may send feedback after registration

How to use AWA

Setup
  1. Install Anki
  2. Install AnkiConnect addon. It allows AWA to communicate with Anki
    1. In your Anki application go to the 'Tools' menu
    2. Click 'Add-ons'
    3. In the opened window click 'Get Add-ons'
    4. Copy and paste '2055492159' (without quotes) inside the 'code' field and click 'OK'
    5. Restart Anki
  3. Open AWA's main page
    1. Anki will ask you to allow access to it. Click 'Yes'.
    2. In AWA choose a deck, where your AWA cards will be stored and click 'Select'
Usage
  1. Type English word inside 'Word' field
  2. Put the context in which the word was found inside the 'Context' field (for example a sentence in a book you're reading). You can skip it, but I highly recommend not to do it because adding your unique context to the word helps to remember it
  3. Click 'Get information' button (or press enter inside the 'Word' field) and wait until translations and other information is downloaded
  4. Click 'Create card' button
Changing the style of the cards
If you're an experienced user, you may want to change the look of the cards by changing the templates for front and back sides of the card as well as additional styling. The one thing you better not to do is changing field names or deleting them. AWA creates a new note type if it cannot find all the fields it needs.
